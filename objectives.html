Upanyasa, in Sanskrit means an exposition

Objectives

  • To study the influence and utility of ever changing technologies on other disciplines
  • To exploit these technologies for better understanding in these disciplines
  • To connect with people who are similarly inclined to pursue these through open and public presentations
  • To share the excitement in the above with others and to learn from their own experiences
  • To establish a platform to take up such studies and to disseminate their outcomes
