Who are we?

Upanyasa is a group of professionals from diverse areas of arts, humanities, science, technology and mathematics. We are storytellers and ambassadors of Science, Culture, Technology and History. We currently explore the influence of Internet of Things, Analytics, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence (AI) on diverse disciplines of human interests such as music, education, employment, conservation, life styles and habits of today’s population. We conduct events that share results of our scientific explorations and findings.

These events provide a platform for likeminded individuals from different backgrounds to come together, debate with openness, converge on issues of importance, and take up action for a better future.
